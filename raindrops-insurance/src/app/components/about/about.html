User Guide - How to Use

1
Browse Insurance Plans

Navigate to the "Insurance Plans" section to view all available health insurance plans with detailed descriptions.

2
Answer Health Questions

When you select a plan, you'll be prompted with a series of questions about your age, health conditions, and lifestyle to calculate your premium.

3
View Premium Amount

Based on your answers, the system calculates and displays your personalized premium amount along with plan details.

4
Enter Personal Details

Fill in your personal information including name, contact details, and address. All fields are validated for accuracy.

5
Make Payment

Enter your payment details securely. Choose your preferred payment mode and frequency (yearly, half-yearly, quarterly, or monthly).

6
Confirmation

Receive instant confirmation of your insurance subscription with all policy details.
